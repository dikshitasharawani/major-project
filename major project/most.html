School Of Management

School of Engineering and Management offers a wide range of UG and PG programmes. All our courses within the programmes prepare the students to be industry oriented and increase their employability. The teaching pedagogy used within curriculum enhances learning and orients students to have an analytical as well research-oriented outlook towards business world.

School of Management is a much sought for students aspiring to do an MBA program.
MBA Program is accredited by NBA for a period of 3 years starting from 2023-24.
To be of choice among aspiring managers to launch successful careers and engage in research as a means of keeping abreast of latest developments and generate new knowledge in management.
Our motive is to:
Develop industry ready and effective managers by imparting insightful knowledge and necessary skills.
Provide an environment to aid continuous learning among students.
Collaborate with industry for creating meaningful opportunities for students.
Continuously strive to increase and improve research activities.
• Create and leverage opportunities in training and consultancy to foster linkages with industry.
Create new businesses, transform finance, and explore a world of opportunity at the speed of markets.br

The MBA program is of 4 semesters (2 years) duration. The evaluation of students comprises of both continuous assessment and an end-semester exam wherever appropriate. A variety of assessment tools are employed to facilitate wholesome learning.

The first year comprises of foundation and professional core courses to enable students gain a width of knowledge across the important functions of management.
The program leads to a dual specialization in the second year. A variety of electives are included to enable students attain both “width” and “depth” in their specializations.

The curriculum includes a two-month summer internship in a company on completion of the first year. This provides an opportunity to the student get a real experience of the corporate world, experience the application of concepts and take up the study of a problem. The final semester has a three month project-internship which will require the student to carry out an in-depth study with a research orientation. Apart from these, many of the courses have a project component.The students are therefore “industry ready” when they complete the program.
