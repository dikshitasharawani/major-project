School Of Engineering

School of Engineering and Technology offers a wide range of UG and PG programmes. All our courses within the programmes prepare the students to be industry oriented and increase their employability. The teaching pedagogy used within curriculum enhances learning and orients students to have an analytical as well research-oriented outlook towards business world.

our Engineers don’t just make things – we make things possible. Build innovations that drive our business and financial markets worldwide. Create new businesses, transform finance, and explore a world of opportunity at the speed of markets.
